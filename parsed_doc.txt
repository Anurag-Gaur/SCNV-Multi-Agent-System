Executive SummaryThe SCNV Agent (Supply Chain Network Visibility Agent) is an AI-powered, autonomous multi-agent system designed to monitor, classify, and optimize Stock Transfer Orders (STOs) in real-time within a brewery/FMCG supply chain network. The system addresses a critical business problem: a significant percentage of inter-location stock movements are "unproductive" — they do not add measurable business value, inflating transport costs by millions of dollars annually while consuming product shelf life.Key architectural highlights of this final version:5-Layer Architecture: Perception → Agent Core → Memory → Tools → Action5 Specialized Agents communicating via A2A (Agent-to-Agent) protocolDual-Mode Communication Agent: Push notifications (Slack/Teams) + Pull Q&A (RAG chatbot)Pluggable Celonis Process Mining Agent: Toggle ON/OFF per client deploymentTwo-Tier Classification: Deterministic rules (~80%) + LLM reasoning (~20%)Key Business ImpactMetricCurrent StateTarget with SCNV AgentUnproductive STO Rate (UK)14%< 5%Unproductive STO Rate (Belgium)9%< 3%Customer Volume from Sub-optimal Source (UK)29%< 10%Transport Cost Savings (EUR Zone)—~$2M/yearGlobal Savings Potential (7 countries)—~$22.8M/yearTime: STO Created → Corrective ActionNext-day (batch)< 5 minutesAnalyst Manual Effort~4 hrs/day< 30 min/day2.
