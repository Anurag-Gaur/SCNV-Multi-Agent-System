SCNV AGENT
Supply Chain Network Visibility Agent
1. Executive Summary
The SCNV Agent v2.1 architecture document provides a strong foundation for classifying and correcting unproductive Stock Transfer Orders (STOs). However, a detailed review against operational dashboards and business requirements reveals significant gaps in three critical areas that prevent the system from delivering full value:
Allocation Efficiency Monitoring: No agent or KPI tracks whether customer Sales Orders are being shipped from the optimal (strategic) source plant.
Country-wise Volume Tracking: All KPIs in the current doc are global. There is no mechanism to measure productive vs. unproductive transfer percentages at the country level.
Pre-shipment Intervention: The system triggers on STO creation but misses the Delivery / Picking trigger window — meaning allocation errors at planning time go uncorrected.

4b | Plant → Plant (Dual Source, any direction) | MISSING | SKU produced at both plants | Destination can self-produce — transfer is waste
